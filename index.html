About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student at the University of Michigan majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. I am working with Professor Honglak Lee's group in researching deep reinforcement learning (DRL) algorithms for learning to perform tasks. I am interested in the intersection of DRL and robotics. My long term career goal is to develop intelligent robots that are capable of performing complex tasks.


Recently, I was a SWE intern on Google Ads working on serving Google's recommendation models at scale. Prior to that, I worked at Luminar, a self-driving car startup on building a data processing and labeling pipeline. I also worked at an education tech startup, Socratic (acquired by Google), on an open-sourced math equation parser and solver library.
