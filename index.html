I am a first-year Robotics Masters student at the University of Michigan (UM) advised by Prof. Honglak Lee. My current research focuses on developing data-driven algorithms for sequential decision-making. I am interested in deploying these algorithms on real-world robotic systems.

I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at UM (2017 - 2020). I've completed industry internships at Luminar, Google Ads, and Invisible.ai. This past summer, I worked with Prof. Changliu Liu at the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. My research focused on developing a safe control architecture for self-driving in dynamic environments.

I am actively looking for PhD positions starting in Fall 2021 and summer 2021 internships.
